About The Book

The book "EverySingle" is a guide to incorporating prayers and confessions into your daily life, particularly focusing on relationships and marriage. It offers practical advice, personal testimonies, and effective prayers to help you navigate the journey to finding your life partner and experiencing the power of prayer in your marital destiny.
